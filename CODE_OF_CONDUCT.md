# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The OpenGRC RMF Framework is committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and professional environment for collaboration. We welcome contributions from individuals of all backgrounds and experience levels.

We expect all participants to engage respectfully and constructively.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Communicate respectfully and professionally
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on technical and architectural discussion
- Respect differing viewpoints
- Maintain confidentiality where applicable

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks
- Disruptive or inflammatory comments
- Sharing confidential or proprietary information
- Any conduct that undermines a professional collaboration environment

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove or restrict contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting Issues

Concerns may be reported via GitHub Issues or directly to the project maintainer.
